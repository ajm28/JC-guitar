Who am I?

Hi, I'm Jim. A guitarist & guitar teacher based in Stirling. I teach 1:1, groups and online via Zoom. I have been playing guitar for over 30 years & teaching for over 20 years. Played in many live bands, done session work and featured on multiple albums.

Lessons are fun and friendly, and are structured to meet the individual's needs as well as offering a comprehensive teaching syllabus from beginners to advanced, with a view to you becoming a versatile & well-rounded guitarist with the knowledge & ability to create your own style & sound. I also have stacks of ideas, lessons & exercises to help you out of creative ruts or to overcome any technique hurdles, or simply learn the songs you've always wanted to play, whilst helping you understand the theory & techniques behind them.

Lessons are available on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays & Thursdays on a weekly, fortnightly, or ad-hoc basis.
